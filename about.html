Hi!



We are Group SDG13_T18_G53, in Poly Forum 2021


We created this project, which is currently in the pitching process, to foster a deeper understanding of the ecosystem in Singaporeans who are not aware of the domino effect the way they interact with wild animals has on the local ecosystem.

As a result, we hope that everyone who visits our website leaves with the ability to empathize with the local wildlife, which is in danger due to having their habitats taken away for urban development.

Thank you!
